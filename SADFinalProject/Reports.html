CASE STUDY #24

From Paper to Cloud: Digitizing Barangay Records

The Challenge: Barangay San Roque relied entirely on physical logbooks, leading to data loss during flood season.
The Solution: CCMS Extension deployed a custom, offline-first record system and trained 15 officials.
The Impact: 100% of records digitized, reducing retrieval time from 30 mins to 30 seconds.
