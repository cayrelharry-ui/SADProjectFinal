Bridging Technology & Community

Welcome to the CCMS Extension Services. We are the community engagement arm of the College of Computer & Multimedia Studies, dedicated to transferring technological expertise, promoting digital literacy, and fostering sustainable development through collaborative partnerships.

Vision

"CNSC as a premier higher education institution in the Bicol Region providing excellent services and developing graduates with highest ethical standards."

Mission

The Camarines Norte State College shall provide higher and advanced studies in the fields of education, arts and science, economics, health, engineering, and management.

Core Objectives

  • ✔ Deliver relevant technical skills training.
  • ✔ Promote sustainable community development.
  • ✔ Establish strong linkages with LGUs.
  • ✔ Empower stakeholders through ICT.
