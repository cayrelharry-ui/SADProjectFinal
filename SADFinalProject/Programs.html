Ongoing ICT Training

Barangay Digital Literacy Program

Rationale

In the modern era, digital literacy is no longer a luxury but a necessity. Many barangay officials in coastal areas of Camarines Norte lack the technical skills to efficiently manage records and access national government portals. This project aims to bridge that gap to improve local governance and individual employability.

Objectives

  • To train 100% of barangay secretaries in basic MS Office applications.
  • To introduce cloud storage solutions for disaster-resilient record keeping.
  • To educate youth on safe internet practices and cyber hygiene.

Expected Outcome

A fully digitized barangay record system and a community that is capable of utilizing online government services (eGov PH) effectively.
